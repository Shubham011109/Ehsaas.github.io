    Ehsaas is an NGO aimed at providing clean and safe water to people living in developed countries. This organization prides itself on transparency; funds received through donations are used to sponsor safe water projects. In addition, all projects are proven via the use of photos and GPS, making it possible for donors to see how their money is being spent. Their mission is not only to solve the water crisis in developing countries but to reinvent charity. With approximately 28,389 water projects in about 26 developing countries, Ehsaas has provided safe and clean water to over 8 million people.
